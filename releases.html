Gleam - releases by date

(Hover each Lucy for release note)

Gleam has had 51 releases over 2566 days. That's an average of 51 days between releases, with a median of 48 days between releases.

I have not looked into what changed in each release, but my sense is many releases improved tooling quality. Keeping the language itself relatively simple saves time and effort for tooling, and makes tooling easier to build.
